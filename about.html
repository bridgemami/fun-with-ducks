The Choice of The Dabbling Duck

The dabbling duck is my favorite animal. In a close second is the dog. I am not sure if there is any logic to the choice. The dabbling duck has very beautiful and diverse colors. Additionally, you can find dabbling ducks all over the world.

Due to this project, I have learned more about the identity and sub-species of ducks.
